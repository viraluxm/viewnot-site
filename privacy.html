Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 3, 2026

Viewnot ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Viewnot Chrome extension and the viewnot.com website. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it. By using Viewnot, you agree to the practices described here.

1. What Data We Collect

Viewnot collects the following data when you use the extension during a Whatnot live show:

  • Live show analytics data — orders, units sold, sale prices, timestamps, and show identifiers from your active Whatnot live show
  • Host names — names you configure manually in the extension settings
  • Buyer usernames — publicly visible Whatnot usernames associated with individual purchases during your show
  • Show metadata — show start and end times, and the livestream ID from Whatnot's platform

We do not collect passwords, payment information, personal financial data, or any information from Whatnot pages that are not active live shows.

2. How We Collect It

The Viewnot extension reads real-time event data from your active Whatnot live show page. This is done by monitoring the Whatnot seller dashboard page you are already viewing — we do not access any other websites or browser tabs. Data is only collected when you actively start a tracking session by clicking "Start" in the Viewnot prompt.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect exclusively to:

  • Display real-time performance metrics to you during your live show
  • Store your show history so you can review past performance
  • Generate per-host performance breakdowns (Pro plan)
  • Export show data to Google Sheets at your request (Pro plan)

We do not sell your data to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising. We do not share your data with any party other than those necessary to operate the service (our database provider, Supabase).

4. Data Storage

Your show analytics data is stored securely in our database hosted by Supabase, a third-party database provider. Supabase stores data in secure, encrypted infrastructure. You can review Supabase's privacy practices at supabase.com/privacy.

Extension settings (host names, preferences) are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's built-in storage and do not leave your device unless you are on the Pro plan with Supabase sync enabled.

5. Data Retention

We retain your show analytics data for as long as your account is active. Free plan data is retained for 7 days. Pro plan data is retained indefinitely until you delete it or close your account.

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at hello@viewnot.com.

6. Third-Party Services

Viewnot integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — database storage for show analytics data
  • Google Sheets — optional export feature (Pro plan only, requires your explicit action)

We do not integrate with advertising networks, social media tracking, or analytics platforms that track you across other websites.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@viewnot.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Children's Privacy

Viewnot is intended for use by Whatnot sellers and is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of Viewnot after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
